J3M.

Each diamond has their own stories of their background. All so mesmerizing and unique that is valued and chershied by many; just like us; Janet, Michelle, Mayra, and My, J3M. Coming from different races and circumstances, we have invested our time to the sisterhood.

Why J3M?

J3M represents the first initial of our names because there is one J and three M's in J3M: Janet, Mayra, Michelle, My.

Girls Who Code

We are part of the 2018 Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Royal Bank of Canada! The seven week program made our hidden talents shine like crystals. Not only did we learn about computer science, we also experienced the importance of sisterhood and the ways women empowered the world. The program shined light onto various social injustices, and provided the tools to help solve world class problems.
