Girls Who Code

We are part of the 2018 Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Royal Bank of Canada! The seven week program made our hidden talents shine like diamonds. Not only did we learn about computer science, we also experienced the importance of sisterhood and the ways women empowered the world. The program shined light onto various social injustices, and provided the tools to help solve world class problems.
